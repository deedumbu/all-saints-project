Get Involved

All saints acknowledges the "ministry of all believers", which basically means that the responsibility for spreading and growing God's kingdom on earth lies with all of us not just the ordained.

We offer a wide variety of ministries where parishioners can offer their talents and time.To get involved in any of hte ministries, please email the Church Office for more information.

All Saint Men's Fellowship

During this time where we don't know what tomorrow holds, it is important for us mento have a community where we can share thoughts and encouragement about the challenges that we face in our daily lives

It will also help us to network and share God's word and what direction he wants us to focus on as the men of All Saints.

With this mind, we are starting an All Saint Men's Fellowship Group, which for now will be on Whatsapp platform. To join this ministry, please message Joreme Samuel at 0828922889 with your name, or send a message to the Church WhatsApp number 066 132 7755.

Once we have the group created, we will share some thoughts, ideas and next steps.
